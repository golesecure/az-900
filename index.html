What is AZ-900?

The Microsoft Azure Fundamentals (AZ-900) certification is an entry-level credential designed to validate foundational knowledge of cloud services and how those services are provided with Microsoft Azure. This certification is ideal for individuals who are beginning their cloud journey or looking to understand the basics of cloud computing.

Exam Details:
Duration: 60 minutes
Questions: 40-60 questions
Format: Multiple choice, multiple response, and scenario-based
Passing Score: 700 out of 1000

Who Should Take This Course?

This certification is perfect for:

  • IT professionals starting their cloud career
  • Business stakeholders who want to understand Azure capabilities
  • Students and recent graduates entering the tech industry
  • Sales and marketing professionals working with cloud products
  • Anyone interested in cloud computing fundamentals
  • Developers wanting to understand Azure services
